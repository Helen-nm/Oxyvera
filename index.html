Facts about Air Pollution

Almost nowhere has clean air

Less than 1% of land has safe air.
Almost no one breathes truly clean air

Air pollution kills many people

1 in 10 people die from sickness caused by
dirty air. In 2017, it caused about 5 million deaths.

It shortens how long people live

Dirty air cuts lives short more than smoking or war.
People in India live about 6 years less due to it.

It costs a lot of money

Air pollution costs the world nearly $3 trillion.
This is from sick days, asthma, and early births.

Poor countries suffer the most

People in poor countries are 100 times more likely to
die from dirty air than those in rich countries.

Only 7 countries have safe air

Just 7 countries met clean air rules in 2023. Bangladesh
and Pakistan had pollution 14 times above safe limits.
