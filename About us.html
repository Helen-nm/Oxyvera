Our Mission

“Our mission is to raise awareness about air pollution and promote sustainable smart city solutions through education, innovation, and responsible daily actions. We aim to encourage people to make eco-friendly choices that help create cleaner air and a healthier urban environment.”

Our Vision

“Our vision is to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable smart city where technology and community work together to reduce air pollution, improve air quality, and build a better future for everyone.”


We encourage our community to take action


Our team created this website to spread awareness about air pollution and its effects on health and the environment.

We hope our project encourages people to make smart daily choices for cleaner air.
